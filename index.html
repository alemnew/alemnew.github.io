Bio

Alemnew Sheferaw Asrese is Doctoral student at the department of Communications and Networking, Aalto University, Finland. He is working under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Joerg Ott and Dr. Pasi Sarolahti. His doctoral study is fully funded by the European Commission Marie Curie Initial Training Network program, under the METRICS project.

He received Master degree (2014) in Computer Science from University of Trento, Italy where he was advised by Prof. Alberto Montresor and his Bachelor's degree (2010) in Information Science from Adama Science & Technology University, Ethiopia. He was a visiting PhD student at Chair of Connected Mobility of Technical University of Munich (2017), INRIA - Paris, France (2017), Simula Research Laboratories (2016). His current research focus is network measurement and QoE modelling. Before joining Aalto, he was intern student at NEC Europe Labs, Germany, a developer at GreenPrefab Italia, Italy, a graduate assistant at Adama Science & Technology University, Ethiopia.
